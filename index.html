Why knife sharpening?

the benefits

sharpening is fun!

Even though it always comes down to the results, sharpening is a fun thing to do. While some love to cook, there are others who enjoy sharpening their kitchen knives. It feels amazing to use a knife you have sharpened yourself.

know your knife

You'll form a special connection with your knife. Discovering your knife's specific character, the shape of it's edge and how hard or soft the steel is.

You save money

Sure buying the materials needed can be an investment. But what about sending your knvies in to be sharpened? With a couple of what stones your set for years. In the long run sharpening your knives yourself is far cheaper.
